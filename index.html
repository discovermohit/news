Gartner's Monday read: 40% of enterprise apps embed AI agents this year — but 40% of projects may not survive

Gartner forecasts that 40% of enterprise applications will embed task-specific AI agents by the end of 2026 — up from under 5% in 2025 — while simultaneously warning that more than 40% of agentic-AI projects are at risk of cancellation by 2027. Translation: agents are arriving fast, but a lot of them won't earn their keep.

  • Agent adoption: <5% → 40% of enterprise apps in a single year.
  • The catch: 40%+ of agentic projects at cancellation risk by 2027.
  • Causes: weak ROI, governance gaps, escalating cost.
  • Production AI is now live at 72% of enterprises.
Why it matters:A 40%-in / 40%-out split is the signature of a hype curve crossing into reality — the winners won't be those who deploy the most agents but those who can kill the ones that don't pay, which makes ROI discipline, not ambition, the differentiator for H2 budgets.
